OUR STORY

25+ Years of Carpentry & Roofing Mastery

Founded on the principles of precision craftsmanship and structural longevity, Cyprian De Creative Carpenter has grown from a local carpentry workshop into Nigeria's premier architectural roofing and structural service company.

We combine time-honored woodworking techniques with high-end, weather-proof technological roofing systems. Our team is passionate about building roofs that form a perfect shield and elevate the overall curb appeal of residential and commercial properties.

Our Core Mission

To design, formulate, and execute stellar architectural roofs that ensure 100% weather safety and lasting beauty.

Our Vision

To remain the top-tier structural roofing partner in West Africa, trusted for unparalleled safety metrics and high-end materials.

CREDENTIALS & COMPLIANCE

Uncompromising Safety Standards

Roofing requires precision, care, and total adherence to safety protocols. We invest continuously in workforce certification, strict site boundaries, and OSHA safety guidelines.

OSHA Compliant

Every on-site roofer wears comprehensive safety gear, harnesses, and follows strict fall-protection procedures.

Licensed Builders

Certified carpentry professionals registered with national engineering and craftsmanship associations.

Material Warranty

Exclusive partnerships with leading structural timber and tile suppliers to guarantee lifetime warranties.

Fully Insured

Comprehensive general liability insurance covering active worksites for complete client peace of mind.
