SPECIALTY 01

Roof Replacement

When wear and tear compromises structural reliability, a full-scale replacement is the safest, most financial-saving path forward. We strip down weather-worn surfaces, evaluate foundational carpenter framing, and construct custom roofs from the ground up.

Our team works with architectural slate tiles, premium asphalt layouts, and structural metal solutions, ensuring a beautiful, robust roof with custom flashing that delivers decade-spanning peace of mind.

  • Premium Shingles & Asphalt Solutions
  • Natural Structural Slate Selection
  • Custom Flashing & Ventilation Integration

SPECIALTY 02

Maintenance & Repair

Small roof damages shouldn't translate to extensive home interior replacements. Our dispatch repair crews are quick to address wind-blown shingles, damaged skylight seals, gutter blockages, and chimney flashing gaps.

Through systematic inspection reporting, we identify immediate stress points and apply high-grade architectural sealants and secure carpentry supports to safeguard your structural envelope.

  • Wind Damage & Shingle Adjustments
  • Chimney Base & Flashing Repair
  • Rapid Emergency Leak Patching

SPECIALTY 03

Water Proofing

High tropical downpours demand premium liquid or membrane sealing protections. We specialize in flat roof sealing, custom chemical coatings, and deep concrete roof waterproofing solutions.

Our specialized system forms a seamless protective barrier over your substrate, guaranteeing that water can never penetrate your building core or weaken foundation concrete works.

  • Liquid Sealing & Bitumen Membranes
  • Complete Flat Roof System Sealing
  • 10-Year Certified Leak-Free Guarantee
Secure Waterproofing Quote
SPECIALTY 04

Roof Restoration

Restore faded luster and structural beauty without incurring total replacement fees. We clean weather-stained tiles, replace rotting support joints, coat materials in UV reflective membranes, and re-tile weak zones.

This intensive restorative service extends active roof lives by up to 15 years, enhances environmental efficiency via reflective heat barriers, and restores original curb value beauty.

  • Rotting Carpenter Joint Replacement
  • Anti-Fungal Clean & Chemical Coating
  • UV-Reflective Layer Installations
