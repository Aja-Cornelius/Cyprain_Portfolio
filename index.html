QUESTIONS

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the typical scope of your roofing services?

We provide full-spectrum services including custom residential carpentry roofing, slate matching, high-performance waterproofing systems, emergency flashing fixes, and complete post-storm replacements.

Do you handle immediate emergency repairs for leaks?

Yes, we have a rapid dispatch team ready to provide high-wind structural supports and emergency moisture barriers to prevent serious inner home leakages.

What are your waterproofing leakage warranties?

We provide a certified leak-free warranty up to 10 years on fully sealed new installations and structure replacements using premium membranes.

What timeline estimates can I expect for replacements?

Standard home replacements typically require 3 to 5 business days, pending optimal climate windows. We ensure site-cleans happen continuously.

What premium materials do you offer?

We offer weather-treated structural pine timber, premium slate tiles, natural copper fittings, architectural grade dimensional shingles, and heavy-duty waterproofing materials.
